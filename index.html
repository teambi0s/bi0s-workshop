About Us

Team bi0s from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a well-established group of computer security enthusiasts focusing on various areas including but not limited to Web application security, Computer forensics, Binary exploitation, Reverse engineering, Cryptography and Android security.

bi0s is largely a CTF team actively taking part in the international CTFs round the year besides carrying out research on security issues in the real world. Fuelled by the urge for community service, bi0s has been organizing workshops and educating the masses about the need for cyber security.

What participants gains

Being #1 CTF team in India, Team bi0s is highly motivated towards spreading Cyber Security Awareness, especially in India and the team will give its best to make sure that it happens. There are various benefits participants will be getting:

  • Individual Attention
    • We have kept the registration limit to 30 participants only, to give proper attention to the people who attend our workshop. This, in fact, is suggestive by the 5:1 ratio of participants and mentor.
  • Access to our private repository
    • Our private repository of a curated list of training material and challenges to practice in a particular domain.
  • Communication
    • We highly encourage communications to clear the doubts of students, and so we will always be available to clear your doubts with the help of mailing lists.
  • Experience
    • Members of team bi0s are highly experienced in various fields of Security and therefore we won't leave any stone unturned to make sure that our training/talks are accurately conveyed to all kinds of participants.
